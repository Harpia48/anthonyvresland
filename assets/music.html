EPICON

EPICON is a cinematic music project inspired by ancient Greek hymns, Orphic symbolism, and archetypal myth. Each composition is crafted as a sonic ritual — invoking courage, clarity, remembrance, and inner alignment.

From Orphic invocations to epic orchestral journeys, EPICON explores sound as a vehicle for transformation — echoing ancient wisdom through modern production.
